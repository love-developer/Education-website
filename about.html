My Journey

Turning Code Into Creative Solutions

I’m Love Kumar — a passionate Website Developer with over 3+ years of experience building high-performance websites and web apps for global clients. From Shopify to React.js, my focus has always been delivering clean, scalable, and user-focused digital experiences.

2020

Started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Built my first freelance landing page project.

2021

Mastered frontend frameworks like React.js and Tailwind CSS. Delivered 100+ responsive websites.

2022

Expanded into Shopify and WordPress. Became a top-rated seller on Fiverr and worked with international brands.

2023–Present

Over 600+ projects delivered. Now focused on building premium Shopify sections, React dashboards, and portfolio websites like this one.

My Mission

To help individuals and businesses bring their ideas to life through powerful, responsive, and custom-built websites that perform flawlessly across all devices.

My Vision

To become one of the most trusted names in freelance web development by consistently exceeding client expectations with every project I deliver.

What I Stand For

Clean Code

Maintainable, scalable code that ensures long-term stability and performance.

Speed & Performance

Optimized websites that load fast and work smoothly on every device.

Creativity

Every project is tailored with a blend of strategy, design, and functionality.

Client Satisfaction

I deliver what I promise — on time, pixel-perfect, and with clear communication.
