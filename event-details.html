Annual Science & Innovation Expo

Explore the wonders of science, creativity, and innovation at our Annual Science & Innovation Expo. This event is designed to showcase the cutting-edge ideas and talents of our students and faculty, bringing together technology, education, and community in one exciting afternoon.

Join us for engaging exhibits, insightful lectures, and dynamic competitions that highlight the latest in robotics, quantum computing, and student-led experiments. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a curious learner, or an industry expert — this expo promises something remarkable for everyone.

Event Highlights

  • Live science demos by students and researchers
  • Expert session by Dr. Robert Jenkins on quantum technologies
  • National-level robotics competition with exciting prizes
  • Interactive booths on AI, sustainability, and renewable energy
  • Student startups and innovations showcase
